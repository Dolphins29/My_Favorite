Gamepage : Solitaire

Welcome to my gamepage.

Here you can find some information about my favorite game : Solitaire

This game is played with a 52 card deck where the cards are played randomly to organize each of the thirteen cards in each suit in increasing order.

This game is a great way to spend time as it allows you to use your thinking skills.

Solitaire has evolved from 4 forms: Card, marble, canfield, and spider!
